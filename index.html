Why test for
Johne's & BVD?

Both diseases are endemic across much of New Zealand and cause significant economic losses — often before any visible signs appear. Early detection is the most cost-effective form of protection.

  • Johne's can shed in faeces for years before clinical signs emerge
  • A single BVD PI animal can infect and suppress immunity across your whole herd
  • Reproductive failures, poor growth rates, and culling costs accumulate quickly
  • Knowing your herd status enables confident, evidence-based management decisions
  • Testing supports biosecurity when buying or selling stock
